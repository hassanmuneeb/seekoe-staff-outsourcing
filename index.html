Common Questions

Answers to common questions about building a remote team with Seekoe.

Can your remote professionals work with my in-house team?

Yes. Our professionals plug into existing workflows, tools, and managers so they feel like an extension of your in-house team.

How easy is it to scale support up or down?

You can scale your support based on workload, seasonality, or growth plans without long hiring cycles.

Can your team work in my business hours?

Yes. We align schedules around your preferred hours and coverage needs, including overlapping shifts when required.

How do you ensure quality and reliability?

We screen for communication, role fit, and professionalism, then support onboarding and ongoing performance management.

What tools can your team use?

We work inside the tools your business already uses, including CRMs, help desks, project management platforms, calendars, and communication tools.

Can I customize the services I need?

Absolutely! We offer flexible plans, including full-time, part-time, and custom solutions, tailored to your specific business requirements.

How do we communicate with our remote team?

Most clients use a mix of email, Slack, shared task boards, and scheduled check-ins. We adapt to the communication rhythm that already works for your team.
