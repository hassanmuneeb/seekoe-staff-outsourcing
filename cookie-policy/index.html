Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 01, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how AgentAdd LLC (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website ../index.html (“Website”). It explains what cookies are, what types we use, why we use them, and how you can control or disable them.

For more information on how we handle your personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) by a website when you visit it. They help websites remember your preferences, enhance functionality, and collect analytical data to improve performance.

Cookies can be:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser.

  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until manually deleted or they expire.

Cookies can also be:

  • First-party cookies: Set by the website you’re visiting.

  • Third-party cookies: Set by domains other than the one you’re visiting (e.g., for analytics or advertising).

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies on our Website:

a. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These are essential for the Website to function properly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions made by you such as logging in, filling out forms, or setting privacy preferences.

Examples:

  • Session management

  • Security/authentication

  • Load balancing

b. Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the Website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or third-party providers whose services we’ve added to our site.

Examples:

  • Remembering login details

  • Language preferences

c. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors interact with our Website, helping us understand usage patterns and improve our content and functionality.

Examples:

  • Google Analytics

  • Heatmaps

  • Session recording tools

d. Targeting and Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites. They do not store directly identifiable personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and device.

Examples:

  • Facebook Pixel

  • Google Ads

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or disable cookies in several ways:

a. Via Cookie Consent Banner

On your first visit, a banner allows you to accept or reject cookies. You can revisit your preferences at any time.

b. Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies you have

  • Delete them

  • Block third-party cookies

  • Block cookies altogether

To find out how, visit your browser’s help documentation.

c. Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor GPC signals as valid opt-out requests under CCPA and CPRA.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that use cookies or similar technologies, including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Meta (Facebook)
  • LinkedIn Ads

  • YouTube embedded content

These providers have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them directly.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal data is collected via cookies

  • Request access or deletion of this data

  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your data

  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at: info@seekoe.com

6. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our use of cookies or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
