FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our Legal Admin & Paralegal Support services.

What is a virtual legal assistant?

A virtual legal assistant is a remote legal support professional who helps with tasks like intake, document drafting, billing, and communication.

What tasks can you outsource?

Client intake, calendar management, legal research, billing, case tracking, document drafting, and e-filing.

Is it secure?

Yes. We use encrypted channels, read-only access, and HIPAA-compliant systems.

What software do you use?

Clio, PracticePanther, LawPay, QuickBooks, Westlaw, Zoom, and more.

How quickly can I hire?

Most clients are matched with a virtual legal assistant within 3–5 business days.

Do I get to choose my assistant?

Yes. You interview pre-screened candidates and pick the one who fits your firm best.

Can you handle litigation support?

Absolutely. From e-discovery to trial prep, our virtual paralegals are litigation-ready.
