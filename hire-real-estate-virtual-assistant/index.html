FAQs

Get answers about hiring, training, and working with Seekoe. 

What does a real estate virtual assistant do?

A real estate VA handles tasks like lead generation, transaction coordination, CRM management, and marketing. They act as your remote right hand, streamlining operations so you focus on sales.

Are your VAs trained in real estate tools?

Yes, Our assistants are certified in MLS, Zillow, DocuSign, and top CRMs. They stay updated on industry software to ensure seamless workflow integration.

How quickly can I hire a VA?

After your consultation, we match you with pre-screened candidates within 72 hours. Most clients onboard a VA in 5–7 business days.

Can I request a different VA if needed?

Absolutely. We offer unlimited replacements until you’re satisfied. Our 97% retention rate ensures matches are typically perfect on the first try.

Is my data secure with an offshore VA?

Yes. We use encrypted tools, NDAs, and HIPAA-compliant protocols to protect client and transaction data at all times.

Do you offer part-time or full-time VAs?

Both. Choose 20 or 40 hour packages monthly. Scale up or down anytime with no penalties.

What industries do your VAs specialize in?

Our team focuses solely on real estate, with expertise in residential, commercial, and property management workflows.
