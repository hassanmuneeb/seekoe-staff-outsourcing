FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our insurance virtual assistants.

What is an insurance virtual assistant?

An insurance virtual assistant is a remote professional specializing in insurance-related tasks, including customer service, policy management, claims processing, and administrative support. They help agencies operate efficiently without the cost of full-time hires.

Are insurance VAs licensed agents?

No, our virtual assistants provide administrative and customer support but do not hold insurance licenses. For licensed tasks, ensure compliance by involving a licensed agent in your team.

Do I get a dedicated virtual assistant?

Yes! You can hire part-time or full-time VAs who work exclusively for you. We match you with the best candidate based on your needs, ensuring consistency and rapport.

Are they trained in the insurance industry?

Absolutely. Our VAs receive specialized training in insurance processes, terminology, and software. They adapt quickly and align with your agency’s workflows.

What if I’m not satisfied with my VA?

We offer replacements at no extra cost. Interview multiple candidates, and if your chosen VA isn’t the right fit, we’ll find you a better match.

How do VAs handle confidential data?

Strict security protocols ensure HIPAA compliance and data protection. All client information is handled securely, with encryption and access controls in place.

Can VAs work in my time zone?

Yes! We provide 24/7 support, adjusting schedules to match your agency’s operating hours for seamless assistance.
